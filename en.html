FAQ
Can I visit without a reservation?

You are welcome to visit without a reservation. However, the “custom pillow / mattress consultation with advanced measurement equipment” and “sleep consultation” are available by reservation only.

For other menus, booking in advance helps us guide you more smoothly.

Which reservation menu should I choose?

We recommend starting with the “custom pillow / mattress consultation with advanced measurement equipment.” It visualizes your full-body skeletal balance and posture, then helps us suggest suitable options from pillows, mattresses, and more.

Can I consult even if I have not decided to purchase?

Yes. You can visit just for a pre-purchase consultation or trial experience. Please feel free to ask us about bedding choices or sleep-related concerns.

How long does a consultation or trial take?

It depends on the menu, but about 30 to 60 minutes is a general guide.

Can I visit with family or a partner?

Yes, you can visit together. For measurement menus, each reservation slot is for one person, so please reserve one slot for each person who would like to be measured.

Can I change the menu after making a reservation?

If your consultation needs change after booking, please let our staff know in store. We will guide you as much as possible depending on the day’s reservation status and your request.

What can I do after adding LINE?

You can receive opening information and campaign updates. Once reservations begin, you can also book your visit through LINE. If you reserve through LINE, we will send reminder notifications as well.

Where can I make a store visit reservation?

Once reservations begin, you can book through LINE or the reservation site. LINE is convenient, but direct booking from the reservation site is also available.

When will store visit reservations open?

The reservation start date will be announced on this site and through LINE. To receive the news as early as possible, please add us on LINE.
